# Mercy | Mudet Real Solution Stockist

## Overview

Hey there! This project is a straightforward web platform designed to help people easily buy Mudet herbal products and sign up to become a Mudet stockist or network marketer. It takes away the guesswork by guiding you through product selection or registration packages, and then streamlines the entire process by moving your details directly to a WhatsApp chat for quick payment verification and order fulfillment. It's all about making natural wellness and business opportunities accessible without any complicated hoops to jump through.

## Usage

Getting started with Mercy is pretty simple, whether you're looking to buy products or become a stockist.

### Buying Products

1.  **Browse and Select:** Head over to the "Shop Products" section on the homepage or the "Purchase Product" page directly. You'll see the available Mudet herbal extracts, like Cinnamon and ARMOR.
2.  **Choose Quantity:** Once you've picked a product, you can easily adjust the quantity using the plus and minus buttons. The total amount updates automatically.
3.  **Enter Details:** Fill out your contact information, including your full name, email, phone number, and delivery address.
4.  **Payment:** You'll see the bank details for payment. You can copy the account number, account name, and bank with a single click.
5.  **Send to WhatsApp:** After filling in your details and making the payment, hit "Send Order to WhatsApp". This will open a WhatsApp chat with Mercy, pre-filled with your order details.
6.  **Verify Payment:** Send your payment proof (receipt image/PDF) directly in the WhatsApp chat. Mercy will then verify your payment and process your order.
7.  **Payment Status:** You'll be redirected to a payment status page where you can upload your receipt or share it again via WhatsApp, and track the status of your order. You can also switch between Naira and Dollar views for payment amounts.

### Registering as a Stockist/Networker

1.  **Choose a Package:** Navigate to the "Register" page. Here, you'll find different startup packages (Lunch, Ignite, Momentum, etc.) with their respective prices, PV points, and product quantities. Select the one that suits you best.
2.  **Provide Your Information:** Fill in your personal details, including your username, full name, gender, date of birth, email, phone, city, and your bank account details for commissions.
3.  **Payment Details:** Similar to product purchases, the bank details for the registration payment are clearly displayed. Copy them to make your payment.
4.  **Complete Registration:** Click "Complete Registration via WhatsApp". This will generate a detailed message with all your registration and package info, ready to be sent to Mercy on WhatsApp.
5.  **Send Payment Proof:** Just like with product orders, send your payment proof (receipt) in the WhatsApp chat for quick verification and activation of your stockist account.

## Features

- **Product Catalog:** Browse and select from a range of genuine Mudet herbal extracts.
- **Direct Ordering System:** Easily place orders for products with quantity selection and dynamic pricing.
- **Stockist Registration Portal:** Register as a Mudet network marketer or stockist by choosing from various entry packages.
- **WhatsApp Integration:** Seamlessly send order and registration details to the administrator via WhatsApp for efficient processing and communication.
- **Payment Status Tracking:** Keep an eye on your order status, with options to upload or share payment receipts.
- **Currency Toggle:** Switch between Nigerian Naira (NGN) and US Dollar (USD) for payment amounts (especially on the payment status page).
- **Client-Side Form Validation:** Ensures all required information is correctly entered before submission, improving data quality.
- **Responsive Design:** A smooth user experience across various devices and screen sizes.
- **Fast & Modern UI:** Built with React and Tailwind CSS for a quick and visually appealing interface.
- **Informative Sections:** Includes sections for "How It Works," "Featured Products," and "Frequently Asked Questions" to help users understand the business and products better.
